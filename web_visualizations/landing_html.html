Latitude Analysis Summary

This website is dedicated to the analysis of how weather various in relation to the equator.

The datasets on the this page were assembled by pulling data on over 500 cities from OpenWeatherMap API. The overall obejective of the analysis was to analyze how weather changes as you get closer or further from the equator.

The visualizations of data on were comprised using Matplotlib. The evaluations were based on the following factors compared to latitude: temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity.The various pages on this site provide the sources data, visualizations, and explanations and descriptions of the data analysis
